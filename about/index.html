A little about us

Universal Cargo Logistics, a world leader in global container shipping. We are a privately owned global organisation operating a network of over 480 offices in 150 countries, employing a team of over 24,000 dedicated individuals.

Our legacy continues with its philosophy to transport goods quickly, efficiently and economically worldwide. Our main objective is for your products to arrive always on time and in good order, we take away the logistics problems away from you, allowing you to concentrate in your business.

Transportation logistics involves more than trucks, trains, boats or planes. Constant communication, information and coordination between parties are crucial factors for the successful movement of goods from origin to destination.

Over 10 years of combined experience in the industry has given Consolidated Shipping & Logistics Group a complete mastery of freight handling techniques, by Air, Ocean or Land. Automated Cargo tracking system, Warehousing facilities, Packing services add value to our service. Understanding the transportation needs of our customers allows us to keep them informed at all times regarding the status of their shipments.
